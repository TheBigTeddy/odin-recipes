Cheese and Ham Toastie


Description

Cheese and Ham Toasties are a winter classic, combining delicious white bread with melted cheese and slices of ham to warm you up in a simple, homely way.

This is another favourite of the Mrs, and due to that, the bread will be thick cut white bread.

Ingredients

  • Thick cut white bread
  • 2 Slices of tasty cheese
  • Thick slice of ham (smoked leg ham ideal)
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter both sides of the white bread.
  2. Add a single slice ham between the two slices of tasty cheese.
  3. Place sandwhich into a grill or jaffal maker.
  4. Cook until cheese has melted and bread is golden and slightly crispy.
