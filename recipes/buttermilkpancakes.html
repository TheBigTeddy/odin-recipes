Buttermilk Pancakes


Description

i

Buttermilk Pancakes are an all time favourite recipe of mine, and something my Girlfriend loves to have on mornings out!

Hopefully, she is reading this in the future, as we make the pancakes together.
I love you so much, and cannot wait to spend more time together!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Plain Flour
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 Cup White Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 3/4 Cups Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 4 tsp Butter For Cooking
  • Sugar Free Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Place flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl, whisk to combine.
  2. Add egg, milk and vanilla. Whisk until lump free, no longer than 30 seconds.
  3. Heat a non stick skillet , use medium heat if you have a strong stove,
    medium high if you have a weak one. Add a tiny bit of butter (about 1/2 tsp)
    swirl to melt. Use paper towel to mostly wipe the butter off
    (this is the trick to avoid a dodgy 1st pancake).
  4. Pour 1/4 cup batter into the middle of the fry pan (I use a slightly heaped
    ice cream scoop with lever, standard is 1/4 cup).
  5. Swirl pan lightly to spread or use the ice cream scoop edge to spread into 11cm circle.
  6. When bubbles rise to the surface, flip and cook the other side until golden.
  7. Use more butter every 2 to 3 pancakes, depends how good your non stick coating is.
  8. Serve with berries for Girlfriend and Sugar-Free Maple Syrup.
