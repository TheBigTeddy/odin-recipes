Keto Phad Thai


Description

Slightly different recipe to the usual Keto Phad Thai that Girlfriend and I make together.
Still should be good ? Have altered some ingredients.

Ingredients

    Main

  • 1 pack of Keto Noodles
  • 1 TBS Oil
  • 1/2 Large Onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 Cup Bean Sprouts
  • 3 TBS Peanuts chopped

    • For Phad Thai Sauce

  • 2 TBS Sugar Free Ketchup
  • 2 TBS Brown Sugar or substitute
  • 2 TBS Fish Sauce
  • 2 TBS Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Make Noodles according to packet instructions.
  2. Whisk all the sauce ingredients into a small bowl.
  3. Stir Fry Garlic and Onion in a large skillet.
  4. Add chicken into the skillet and cook until not pink.
  5. Whisk eggs, and mix into the chicken.
  6. Add sprouts, scallions, noodles, and any extra ingredients to taste.
  7. Add sauce and mix. Serve when combined!
